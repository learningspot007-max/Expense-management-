💱 Meaning and Usage

The currency exchange emoji 💱 shows two arrows in a loop with the dollar sign ($) and yen sign (¥). It's commonly used to talk about converting money, visiting a bank, or discussing different currencies.

💱 in chat is often used when talking about money conversion, exchange rates, or financial transactions. It fits well in messages about travel expenses, investment discussions, or international shopping.
